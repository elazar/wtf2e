Cheat Sheet

Rolls

  • Perception: Wits + Composure with a possible bonus depending on form
  • Initiative: Dexterity + Composure + 1d10

Healing

  • 1 point of lethal every 15 minutes
  • 1 point of aggravated every 4 days
  • 1 point of bashing every turn

Character Creation

  • Quick Reference p. 84
  • Get 2 dots in Rites (Siskur-Dah rite is free)
  • Everyone begins at 7 Harmony and 10 Essence
  • Everyone starts with 1 dot of Primal Urge (don't go above 3)
  • Every gets 1 free dot in the Totem merit
