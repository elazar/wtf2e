STRINGS OF THE HEART (••)

Prerequisites: Storm Lord

Effect: The first trick to making someone do what you want is finding out what they want, and promising it, threatening it, or offering it. Your Storm Lord has a knack for finding that very thing. After a turn scrutinizing her prey, ask his player, “What does your character want most?” Your Storm Lord instinctively knows the answer, even if she doesn't understand the context. “I want Davis's hand in marriage” is more useful if she knows who Davis is, but she doesn't have to know him to know that answer.
