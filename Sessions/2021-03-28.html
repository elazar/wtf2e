Summary

The Pack pursues a Totem spirit toward Belle Island. Paca meets a young Uratha named Little Shadow.

Notes

  • The Pack's headquarters has a broken dinner plate, from a house where domestic abuse took place, for a locus.
  • Elric performs a rite to give them the Siskur-Dah condition to allow them to touch ephemeral prey (in this case the Totem spirit being pursued, Unseen Fire) with natural weapons.
  • Elric and Dade shift into the Shadow and each spend a point of Essence (putting Dade at 6) to shift into Urshul form for ideal speed and perception to pursue the Totem. Paca remains in the material world to pursue the Totem at its last known location there.
  • Paca finds a young boy of about 10 with a wolf spirit guarding him. The boy introduces himself as Little Shadow and can speak First Tongue. Paca discovers that he's Uratha and that this is likely in his territory. Little Shadow seems to know that the Totem spirit has left the area and headed downtown.
  • Elric and Dade have a brief encounter with the Cadillac Company, who allow them to go after they indicate they are on a hunt and just passing through. The two pass a run-down shop that elicits a bad feeling, one urging them to surrender. Dade recognizes it to be a corrupt Essence-draining place in the Shadow, the Shole, that can lure human and spirit alike, and notes its location for future reference. Elric encounters an entity claiming to be his future self, Future Elric, who accompanies the pair.
  • Dade spends another Essence to use Pack Awareness to communicate with Paca and ask that she get a boat and use it to get to Belle Island where Dade and Elric suspect the spirit has gone. Paca arrives at the docks, manages to render a dock worker unconscious, steals her keys, and steals a boat, leaving the other two on the shore to cross the river.

  • Everyone in the Pack takes 1 Beat for it being the first session.
  • Dade takes 1 Beat for steps toward the Aspiration of obtaining a Totem, leaving him with 3 Beats.
