Summary

The Pack encounters a Verge and hunts Su Ubh with John Selbie. Paca discovers that Miguel lured Su Ubh out into the open, allowing the Pack to dispatch him. Clara is reassured by her spirit, then visits Alex and learns something new about him.

Notes

  • Paca, Dade, and Clara each attempt to track Su Ubh. Dade expends 1 Willpower (putting him at 4) for the tracking check (Wits 3 + Survival 1 - 5 + 3 for 1 Willpower), getting 1 success. Paca also gets 1. Clara gets 3 and gains the Invisible Predator Condition (p. 308). Little Shadow likewise succeeds with the group.
  • The Gauntlet is almost nonexistent in the area of town in which the Pack finds itself, allowing Spirit and Flesh to cross without barrier. The Shadow seeps in, causing the Spirit and Flesh to be almost one and the same. Clara and Dade recognize this as a dangerous Verge (see "Gauntlet Strength," p. 101). John Selbie indicates that the Azlu (see p. 203) would be needed to seal it, and are generally hesitant to work with Uratha. The group resolves to leave that matter for another day and proceed with the Hunt.
  • With the help of Unseen Fire, the Pack continues stealthily tracking Su Ubh through the Verge. The Pack hears a voice, but only one of them recognizes it: Paca hears her husband Patrick. The voice cycles from that of a friend of Miguel to a coworker of Paca's. The scent of Su Ubh is very close.
  • Clara accidently steps on a twig, sending a loud cracking sound through the air followed by a palpable silence. After a moment, the Pack hears the flapping of Su Ubh's wings as it flies into view, sees them all, and lands nearby, cocking his head.
  • Su Ubh says in First Tongue, mimicking the voice of Gregory Whistler, "There's nothing for you here. I'm minding my own business." Paca retorts that Miguel was doing the same before Su Ubh kidnapped him.
  • Paca draws her resolver and attempts (with 2 successes) to shoot Su Ubh, but only grazes him. "Now that was a mistake," he says with the voice of Hanzer-gah Ninna. Paca freezes in fear, unable to move (for 3 turns).
  • Clara attempts to take hold of Su Ubh's mind, but he resists her.
  • John Selbie screams in fury, endowing the Pack with a +1 rating to Brawl and Weaponry attacks that also upgrades the damage of such attacks to Lethal.
  • Dade spends 1 Essence (putting him at 7) to shift reflexively into Gauru form, then goes into an all-out attack against Su Ubh, striking him for 3 Lethal damage. Su Ubh screams in surprise and pain in the voice of Michael Stantz.
  • Little Shadow snarls in First Tongue, "Let no sacred place in your territory be violated," reciting his tribal oath. He attempts to use a gift, but fails.
  • Su Ubh screeches, summoning a murder of crows to attack the Pack at his bidding.
  • Clara again attempts to take hold of Su Ubh's mind, but is foiled.
  • The crows converge on Dade, pecking him and dealing 1 Bashing damage.
  • John Selbie attempts to bite Su Ubh, but he dodges.
  • Dade heals the 1 Bashing damage dealt to him by the crows, then swipes at Su Ubh again, but misses.
  • Little Shadow successfully uses his Knotted Paths (p. 129) gift on Su Ubh, entrapping him in the area for a day.
  • Su Ubh attacks Dade, dealing 3 Lethal damage to him.
  • Paca breaks out of her fear, shifts reflexively into Gauru form, and attacks Su Ubh, striking where his wing meets his body and raking across his chest, spilling forth liquid Essence. "But he wanted this," he says in Miguel's voice as he stumbles backward.
  • Clara shifts into Urshul and bites at Su Ubh, dealing 5 Lethal damage. She sinks her fangs into his neck and rips out his throat. Su Ubh screams in torment and surprise using Alex's voice. His control over the crows is released and they disperse. After a moment, he explodes in a flood of black feathers, leaving behind a flood of 20 Essence (Paca takes 1, Dade takes 3, Clara takes 8, Little Shadow takes 2, Unseen Fire takes 1).
  • The Pack hears Miguel's voice: "Mister Collector, sir, is it okay to come out?" Paca, Dade, and Clara revert into Hishu form. "Mom?!" Miguel calls out. "What are you doing out here with strange bird-men?" Paca asks him. Miguel apparently got it into his head that he might convince Su Ubh to stay in one place long enough for the Pack to attack him. Paca's arms drops to her sides, both impressed and deeply concerned. Miguel had also hoped to ascertain the spirit's original reason for kidnapping him.
  • Paca is able to detect that Miguel is himself, but is still affected by Lunacy and not of his normal mind. The Pack notices an ethereal chain attached to his head with Youthful Curiosity on the other end of it, urging him along. Miguel isn't fettered, but has been influenced. Once he sees the Pack, particularly the look on Paca's face, he shudders and withdraws. In First Tongue, he says, "I didn't know! He was just curious, and wanting to know things." Paca yells at him (in First Tongue) to leave. The spirit begins to sob, then turns and runs away.
  • Miguel see the remains of Su Ubh and seems extremely impressed that the Pack killed him. John Selbie hugs Clara, giving her his thanks for making the kill. The Pack retires to Panera for a post-hunt meal together. By the meal's conclusion, Dade has healed the damage done to him by Su Ubh.
  • Clara seems her spirit in twilight, watching and grinning, brushing up against her legs and saying, "Not bad, sister. Not bad at all. I might even call myself jealous. See, this is why what we do is important, and why that Rage is important: so you can take out suckers like that. But, it's still up to you. Regardless of what you do, I love you."
  • By now, it's been 4 days since the mission to the church of Lupus et Fidelis.
  • When Dade returns to his apartment, he does research on matters of Lunacy, the Occult, and psychology in attempt to help Miguel. (Thunder will do her own research and get back to me on this.)
  • Clara goes to visit Alex, who says he's feeling a lot better. He mentions still having sporadic strange dreams. Clara also detects an odd smell to him, one that seems to indicate he is not fully human. She suspects, though isn't certain, that he may be wolf-blooded, similar to what ghouls are for vampires: something inbetween human and Uratha. He is likely immune to Delirium and has his own special abilities. Clara asks if Alex has felt strangely. He seems eager for non-hospital food and curious to know what mischief she's gotten into in his absence. Both seem ready for Alex to return home.
  • Everyone regains 1 Willpower for time spent with respective Touchstones.
