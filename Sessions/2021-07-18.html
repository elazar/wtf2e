Summary

The Pack goes on a Siskur-Dah and faces off against Claimed of the cannibal spirits and two Hounds.

  • Dade takes 6 lethal damage, then both he and Paca into Garou form.

  • Mooth Hirabayu, "Blood Knife," is the name of the Claimed of the cannibal spirits.
