Summary

Clara arranges to meet with Gregory Whistler. The battle between Dade, Paca, and Bridget Byreen culminates in death. The Cadillac Company reels from the events of the night and withdraws. Paca succumbs to Death Rage and attacks Rafeeq Jackson, who descends into Lunacy and suffers a grievous wound.

Notes

  • Between Clara getting the kitten, who she's named Socks and who has bonded to her, and Dade getting into it with Bridget Byreen last session, Clara contacts Dade and obtains the contact information for the secretary of Gregory Whistler from him. She contacts his secretary and books an appointment with him for 10:30 the next morning.
  • We established that Germaine would probably be staying with Little Shadow, either at Dade's or Paca's, on a semi-permanent basis.
  • Dade heals his previous 3 Lethal damage at the start of his turn. He expends 1 Willpower (putting him at 3) and attacks Bridget Byreen with claws again (5 successes), putting her at 6 Lethal. (She would've healed the damage and Blindness tilt from Dade's last attack at the start of her previous turn.)
  • Cheryll is staring with a glazed look in her eyes, seemingly only somewhat aware of what's happening around her. The rest of the Cadillac Company fares about the same. Rafeeq Jackson looks extremely guilty, as though he's committed some unforgivable sin. Joy seems distraught and in disbelief. Tom doesn't seem to be faring any better. None of them are able to act this round.
  • Paca uses the Flanking merit (p. 108) to lower her Initiative and Defense.
  • Dade expends 1 Willpower (putting him at 2) and attacks Bridget Byreen again (2 successes), dealing 4 Lethal and upgrading 3 of her existing Lethal to Aggravated.
  • Bridget Byreen roars in rage. Rafeeq Jackson manages to pick up his gun, level it at Bridget Byreen, and fire (2 successes). Bridget Byreen heals the residual Lethal damage from Dade's attack and Rafeeq Jackson's shot and attempts to attack him, but he ducks out of the way.
  • Cheryll and the rest of the Cadillac Company attempt to enter the building, but can't because the Gauru forms of Dade, Paca, and Bridget Byreen make it impossible to do so. Joy and Tom remain unable to act.
  • Paca rushes Bridget Byreen and attacks her with claws (7 successes, 9 Lethal damage), upgrading the rest of her health track with Lethal.
  • To avoid Death Rage, Dade spends 1 Essence (putting him at 8) and shifts into Urshul. He expends 1 Willpower (putting him at 1) and attacks Bridget Byreen with his teeth, dealing 6 Lethal and upgrading her to 9 Aggravated.
  • Bridget Byreen heals 3 Lethal and attacks Rafeeq Jackson, who she believes killed LeShaun White, with her claws, ripping open his arm and knicking an artery. The rest of the Cadillac Company remains outside.
  • Paca attacks Bridget Byreen with her claws and deals 4 Lethal, putting her at 10 Aggravated. Dade follows suit with his teeth, finally causing her to collapse and shift into a mangled human corpse.
  • Rafeeq Jackson, still bleeding profusely and very much affected by Lunacy, uses his shaky hands to tear off a fragment of his shirt and wrap it around his wound to staunch the flow.
  • Outside, the Cadillac Company feels their new alpha die. Samuel Hanselman, in a voice unlike him, chokes back rage as he vocally threatens whoever is responsible. Their Pack then withdraws from the area.
  • Before she succumbs to Death Rage, Paca realizes that the nearby members of the Night Watch are liabilities and witnesses.
  • Through her link to the Crescent Shadows, Clara suddenly experiences a feeling of dread in relation to them. She tries texting the group chat, asking what's going on and where the others are, but no one responds. She then feels a presence and voice saying, "Follow the trail of death and you will find what you seek." As quickly as it appeared, the presence is gone. She then attempts to speak with spirits, who in a panic direct her to Night Watch HQ where the chaotic battle has taken place. She returns to the Flesh and follows their direction.
  • Paca uses her claws to make a down and dirty attack (p. 165) against Rafeeq Jackson (3 successes). Rafeeq Jackson fires his gun at Paca and hits (1 success). Paca uses her jaws to rip his arm from his torso. Dade manages to not succumb to Death Rage by proxy via Paca.
  • Dade uses Pack Awareness to sense Clara's imminent arrival. He expends 1 Essence (putting him at 7) to reflexively shift into Gauru form and roars at Paca in attempt to intimidate and distract her, but fails.
  • Paca attacks Rafeeq Jackson again. He is in throes of Lunacy and blood loss, unable to defend himself or otherwise act.
  • Clara arrives on the scene and manages to avoid succumbing to Death Rage as well. She attempts to use her Auspice benefit of Darkess Into Light (p. 22) to quell Paca's Death Rage, giving her the Stunned tilt. Paca's Rage recedes and she returns to her normal, if dazed, state of mind, but remembers nothing that occurred while she was in Death Rage. She reflexively downshifts into Hishu form, still tasting blood in her mouth as she sees Rafeeq Jackson grievously wounded, but not yet dead. Paca and Dade manage to avoid a Breaking Point (p. 104) for consumption of flesh and killing a fellow Uratha respectively.
