Summary

Dade, Little Shadow, and Unseen Fire lose the trail of Hanzer-gah Ninna in the junkyard. Paca is cautiously inducted into the Night Watch. The Pack meets at HQ, discusses having Clara join them, confronts a Fettered spirit in the junkyard, and continues attempting to track Hanzer-gah Ninna.

Notes

  • Background music is Kai Engel - Brume.
  • Dade, Little Shadow, and Unseen Fire lose the scent of Hanzer-gah Ninna on the other side of the junkyard. Dade rolls (Wits + Streetwise / Survival) and burns 1 Willpower (putting him at 5), but fails dramatically and gains the Lost Tracker condition (p. 308). Little Shadow shifts into Dalu form and attempts to find the scent as well, but also fails. Dade texts Paca and Clara and asks if they can meet at HQ.
  • Paca is blindfolded by Rafeeq Jackson and led on his arm to the Hunters' hideout.
    • A woman approximately in her 30's with a rather melodious voice and blonde hair comes down the stairs in high heels, who the cell members refer to Joy and. She's identified as the "face" of the group.
    • Tom is fairly muscular and doesn't seem to talk much. He's identified as the "bulldozer" of the group.
    • Cheryll, the dark-haired woman that Paca previously encountered, is the group's intelligence gatherer. Paca had a run-in with her on 2022-01-09.
    • Paca identifies herself to the cell only as "P." She is sworn in to the cell as a member and issued a flashlight until she exhibits her skills and most suitable role within the group.
    • Rafeeq Jackson refers to the cell as the Night Watch. They apparently conduct their activities in an underground survivalist bunker of some sort.
    • While using the building's restroom, Paca encounters a rather large rat, but unfortunately fails to converse with it.
    • Rafeeq leads Paca out of their headquarters, blindfolded again, 30-45 minutes after she was led in. She provides her mobile number of Rafeeq and tells him to call her if needed.
  • The Pack meets at HQ to discuss the findings from the patrol done by Dade, Little Shadow, and Unseen Fire. They discuss the possibility of Clara joining the Pack; Unseen Fire says she will consider what trial Clara will undertake in order to join.
  • The Pack manages to pick up a stale trail of Hanzer-gah Ninna and returns to the junkyard, where they again find the man that Dade encountered the last time he was there, apparently digging through the trash for food.
    • The man apparently doesn't recall how long he's been in the junkyard. When Paca asks his name, he identifies himself as Eddie, and reiterates his self-proclaimed title of "King of the World."
    • While Paca distracts Eddie, Clara and Dade manage to sneak past them and into the junkyard, but Little Shadow steps on a tin can and draws Eddie's attention to his presence.
    • Paca punches Eddie, thinking he's drawing a weapon to hurt Little Shadow, that turns out to be a birdhouse that's now cracked. Paca then jumps on him and wails on him. He reaches up and touches Paca, dealing 5 bashing damage to her as he begins to somehow magically starts to drain the life from her.
    • As Paca overtakes Eddie, a spirit with visible decay that was inhabiting his body exits and becomes visible to the Pack in twilight. Dade sees a link between the two indicating this spirit to be Fettered (p. 190). He shifts into Urshul form and issues a challenge to the spirit, asking him to provide a reason why he shouldn't be killed, as the rest of the Pack joins in, jeering at the spirit and banging nearby trash can lids together as they successfully Banish the spirit to the Shadow.
    • The Pack is able to pick up the trail of Hanzer-gah Ninna again, out of the junkyard and into abandoned (albeit still intact) buildings. We started an extended contested check to pick it up again. Paca is at 0, Dade is at 1, Clara and Little Shadow are at 4.
