Summary

Dade and Paca bring Little Shadow to a safe place to be nursed back to health. David Stanze pays them a visit. Dade kills him to protect the Pack.

Notes

  • Paca and Dade take the car and head back to Paca's place with Little Shadow. As they pass down a stretch of highway near the woods, Dade feels their call bidding him to return soon under threat of compulsion. They reach their destination and lay Little Shadow on the couch, enduring hard-to-read stares from Hanzer-gah Ninna.
  • There's a knock at the door. It's a small dark-skinned man wearing a robe and looking very nervous. Dade sends Paca upstairs with Little Shadow, then goes to the door, noticing that the man smells human and has the look of prey. After some conversation, Dade realizes that he's David Stanze and beckons him inside.
  • Dade chooses to break the Oath of the Moon and reveals the existence of Uratha and his identity as one to David, which shatters him. Paca shows willingness to kill him, but Dade dissuades her, borrows her keys, and takes David with him.
  • Dade calls Gregory Whistler, who expresses that he can't do anything that Dade couldn't do himself; he declines the offer. Paca comes back out and offers to take care of disposing of David Stanze, but Dade declines, saying that he chose to break the Oath and that this should be his responsibility.
  • Dade drives, all the while attempting to find an option that doesn't involve killing David Stanze, and managing to spur David's anger and hatred in the process. In the end, he makes an unconvincing offer to leave the cult and keep a low profile. Dade decides that he can't take the chance, spends 1 Essence (putting him at 7) to shift into Dalu form, quickly mouths an apology, and snaps David's neck. He fails a breaking point roll and his Harmony reduces by 1, putting him at 6.
  • Dade calls Paca, who points him to a tarp and duct tape in the trunk of the car. Dade wraps David Stanze in them, drives to a graveyard by a nearby farm, digs up the bottom of an open grave, and buries him there, then drives back to Paca's to return her keys.
  • The Siskur-Dah condition persists, as the hunt for the Claimed hasn't been completed yet.
  • The Pack prepares to continue their Hunt for Mooth Hirabayu, and potentially the Beshilu if the Hunt takes them into the sewers.
  • Dade spends downtime investigating Alex Cecil and Julian Graves. At one point, he unmistakeably hears his father Michael's voice.
    • Strength + Brawl - 1 success
    • Wits + Composure + Primal Urge - 1 success
    • He spends 1 Essence (putting him at 6) to use Shadow Gaze - 2 successes
    • The spirit's bans: 1) if he fails to draw a human out of their home by the dawn, he can't take them with him. Any uses of mimicry automatically fail, though he can continue to apply them; 2) he is unable to enter a building unless a door or window is open, and can only attempt to lure a single victim for a single night, and can't pursue those individuals again.
    • The spirit grapples and paralyzes Dade, but then silently lets go. Dade feels a flash of fear and annoyance from it, then it vanishes into the Shadow. After a few minutes of paralysis, he shuts the door and no longer hears voices.
