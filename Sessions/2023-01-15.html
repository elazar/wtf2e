Summary

Paca and Miguel talk about Wren and matters of safety. Wren has a haunting vision and is cared for by the Pack before receiving a visit Rebecca, which Dade is informed of by Unseen Fire. Clara makes a decision and begins training with Paca. The threat of retribution from the Cadillac Company looms.

Notes

  • Paca awakens from a restless sleep and calls Miguel. The two meet at home, share some hot chocolate, and talk briefly about the recent incident with Wren before Paca embraces Miguel and retires to her bedroom to sleep.
  • Wren awakens at HQ from a disturbing vision of abstract Uratha-related imagery with a general feeling of dread. They begin drawing what they can remember from their vision, though its reminiscent of a madman's scrawlings.
  • Clara calls Paca, who awakens at this call, and asks her to meet at HQ. Paca tells Miguel that she must leave the house. To ensure his safety, she gives him her revolver and briefly shows him how to use it.
  • Paca makes her way on foot to HQ, with modestly successful parkour maneuvers to increase her speed.
  • Once the Pack has gathered, Clara and Dade explain the incident that occurred earlier in the evening at The Last Firstborn. Given the seemingly dichotomic abilities of Sister Cathwulf and Horace Fenchurch, Clara also mentions her consideration of his offer to work for him, which Paca cautions against.
  • Wren scribbles intently and doesn't respond to prompts from the rest of the Pack. Clara and Dade both examine her drawings and are able to deduce that they're related to Clara's vision from earlier in the evening, which an implication that the relevant situation could spread outside their territory, even globally, if it's not stopped. It's decided that Clara will attempt to infiltrate Horace Fenchurch's organization, with Paca's help to function as an undercover agent.
  • Little Shadow appears at HQ, having been on border patrol and with Youthful Curiosity on his shoulder. Little Shadow reports that the Cadillac Company is likely to take some action, perhaps not that night but soon, against the Pack for the death of their alpha.
  • Youthful Curiosity attempts to greet Wren, but Dade and Paca explain that Wren is in an odd mental state. Youthful Curiosity volunteers to investigate the situation by consulting with other spirits, then promptly vanishes.
  • Having run out of paper, Wren begins laughing maniacally scribbling on random surfaces, includes Clara's arm and Paca's hand (in the latter case writing "the end"). Upon hearing mention of the Cadillac Company, Wren suddenly regains focus, but then faints and collapses. Little Shadow kneels next to them, speaking in First Tongue and trying unsuccessfully to prompt them out of their mental haze.
  • As Paca tries to get Wren onto the couch, she attempts to make casual conversation with Clara and Dade. Once she's successful, she reminds Clara of their meeting the next morning, then departs HQ.
  • Clara confirms that Unseen Fire will look after Wren, then departs with Dade and Little Shadow. The three are eventually greeted by Germaine, who greets them excitedly and speaks animatedly with them as they journey toward Dade's home. Dade attempts to continue English lessons with Little Shadow, but is too tired from the night's events to make any inroads.
  • Wren eventually awakens with the Confused condition (p. 307). They find Rebecca standing nervously outside the warehouse with Unseen Fire baring fangs and bristling fur in her direction, referring to her as an abomination and ushering her out of HQ. Wren attempts to reassure Unseen Fire, who continues to warn Wren against interactions with Rebecca and then begrudgingly withdraws. Rebecca offers to help Wren sleep, which they accept, adding the Steadfast condition (p. 311) for four days. She then nudges Wren in the direction of the couch inside of HQ, encouraging them to look after themselves before waving goodbye and leaving.
  • In the morning, Paca uses her school access to get Clara's address, goes to her apartment, and calls her cell phone to wake her and be let in. She then proceeds to train Clara in how to be cautious in dealing with others, glean information about them and evaluate their veracity, lie convincingly, etc.
  • Dade wakes to someone speaking his name and a wet nose being pushed against his cheek. After a moment, he recognizes the voice as speaking First Tongue, referring to him as "Ithaeur," and opens his eyes to find Unseen Fire, who reports the incident with Wren and Rebecca to him and reminds him that loons are the selected prey of Ithaeur and Bone Shadows. Dade thanks Unseen Fire for bringing the matter to his attention and promises to speak with Wren and to look into ways to protect HQ from loons when Unseen Fire is not present.
  • Wren is able to sleep through the night and awakens with the Confused condition resolved.

Beats

  • Everyone takes 1 Beat for attendance and 5 Beats for the long break between games and to celebrate the game restarting.
  • Wren takes 1 Beat for resolving the Confused condition.
  • Dade takes 1 Beat for locating Sister Cathwulf, even though she managed to disappear again, putting him at 61 Beats.
