Summary

Dade and Gregory Whistler discover a wound in the Hisil. Clara cuts loose and takes advantage of the amenities of the Amber Club. The rest of the pack discovers Paca's absence and reach out to Miguel. Dade and Little Shadow meet with John Selbie.

Notes

  • It's the last day of the half moon.
  • After departing Gregory Whistler's office, Dade realizes there's a different, strong, and foreboding resonance in the Umbra, feelings of violence, torment, and a void that hungers.
    • Dade doubles back and explains the situation to Gregory Whistler. The two travel to a nearby locus, an old scarred tree used in the past for hangings within a graveyard that's fallen out of use, in territory bridging Whistler's own with that of the Cadillac Company. The two cross over to the Hisil. The tree there is twisted, black, and seemingly bleeding sap, unnerving even Whistler.
    • They begin traveling back toward Gregory Whistler's office as he explains that there is a wound in the Hisil. Bale Hounds, Uratha who are neither Pure nor Forsaken but can skillfully disguise themselves as either, delight in watching the world burn and in opening such wounds. Whistler suspects that they might be responsible. Whistler and his son Alek were pursuing one when Alek died.
    • Gregory Whistler suggests that Dade consult with his pack about how to handle this situation, implying that this crisis may be an opportunity to cultivate goodwill with the Cadillac Company. The two part ways, Whistler moving back toward his office still in the Hisil while Dade doubles back to the hanging tree and returns to the Flesh.
  • Having parted ways with Horace Fenchurch and with the $500 bonus he provided, Clara wades into the strong resonance of the Amber Club abounding around her. Using her heightened Uratha senses, she's able to locate two women willing to sell her a drug of her choice. After sufficient indulgence, she moves onto the dance floor and manages to impress and gather a crowd of equally influenced patrons.
  • Having returned to the Flesh, Dade texts the pack, telling them there's an emergency and to meet him at HQ. Little Shadow arrives shortly after Dade; Clara takes a bit longer to orient herself and exit the Amber Club. Dade and Little Shadow meet her halfway and bring her back to HQ.
  • Dade finds the note left by Paca, then has Clara call Miguel. Dade reassures him following his encounter with the police, and tells him he'll come to get him after the pack deals with their present situation. Unseen Fire appears. Dade explains the situation and asks that she watch over Clara, who isn't in the best of states to travel further.
  • Dade and Little Shadow travel back to the graveyard to meet John Selbie and discuss the situation with him. He doesn't know any rituals to mend a wound in the Hisil, but promises to investigate.

Thoughts

    • Star: It was nice and very humanizing to see Clara cut loose a bit in the Amber Club, after being largely focused on Alex and attempting to quell her Uratha nature.
