Summary

Wren receives an unexpected visit from a stranger. Dade discusses the Cadillac Company with Little Shadow and Gregory Whistler and the Night Watch with Paca. Clara meets with Horace Fenchurch and accepts his offer. Paca discovers she is being hunted and disappears.

Notes

  • Wren checks on their garden. They transition into presenting as male and shower in preparation for a date with Mason, who they haven't seen in sometime. There's a knock at the door, and Wren opens it, revealing a woman who is seemingly a member of Lupus et Fidelis. The woman relates knowledge of the events at the Last Firstborn earlier in the evening and expresses the gratitude of Sister Cathwulf for their visit, but also confusion surrounding their reason for visiting and why they're "upset" with her, and claims to have sensed Wren's presence in the process of passing by their location.
    • Wren invites the woman inside to attempt to answer their questions as they're able, and offers her food and drink. They mention the ability of Uratha to consume spirits, information which was previously unknown to Lupus et Fidelis, and ask the woman for more information about the organization, how they came to be, how they know about the Uratha, and so forth.
    • They also tell her of their Cahalith auspice, their nature as a storyteller, the existence of First Tongue, the relevance of the phrase "this story is true" to their auspice, and brief descriptions of other auspices and of Wren's own tribe. In doing so, they break the Oath of the Moon in the process, specifically that "the Herd must not know;" they gain 1 Harmony, placing them at 9 (see p. 96 and 104).
    • Wren describes the nature of Death Rage and the overall hesitation, if not avoidance, of Uratha to kill humans.
    • The woman in turn explains that they view Uratha as children of gods and privy to the Spirit. She mentions a normal human named Diana, who witnessed a battle between two Uratha, made subsequent claim of visions of Uratha, and began Lupus et Fidelis. The goal of the organization was to come to know the Uratha, but given the information that Wren has provided, this might change. She takes her leave of Wren.
  • Dade texts Wren saying that the two should meet and talk at the earliest opportunity, then feeds Little Shadow and Germaine. Little Shadow has been out of the Hisil for over a month now, putting him at Harmony 5. When Dade prompts him about it, he responds in English without realizing it, then hugs Dade in surprise. They discuss making a phone call to his parents that evening so he can speak to them, and maybe arranging for them to meet later in the week. They finish breakfast, then leave to take Germaine for a walk and get ice cream.
    • As they walk, Dade asks Little Shadow about his knowledge and experiences of the Cadillac Company. He recalls LeShaun White as a rather boisterous, charismatic, and ambitious individual, and believes that his death may accelerate their attempt to gain complete control of Detroit.
    • The Pure tribes, with members such as Julian Graves, have something to gain from their weakened state.
    • It might also affect Gregory Whistler, despite his relatively independent status; Dade calls his secretary to schedule a visit with him later that evening.
    • Rumor has it that the Cadillac Company suspects a human Hunter might've been responsible for LeShaun's death. Dade also texts Paca, suggesting that they meet that evening to discuss the possibility.
  • Paca and Clara complete their training when Clara manages to best Paca in a competition of wits, much to Miguel's bemusement.
  • Dade and Paca meet at HQ. Paca admits that the Night Watch acted quickly and killed LeShaun White, and didn't inform her until after the fact. The closest hospital to their HQ is the same one where Alex Cecil was taken.
    • Paca texts the Night Watch, attempting to provoke a response that might facilitate a meeting with them. They only respond that they aren't alright, suffered an attack at the base, and are recovering at the hospital. They reassure Paca that they'll be fine, caution her not to go on a vengeance spree, and agree to meet with her to talk once they've healed. She declines Dade's offer to accompany him to see Gregory Whistler in favor of retiring to her home to rest.
  • Clara goes to the Amber Club and is escorted in to meet with Horace Fenchurch.
    • He has a fresh and apparently themed spread including raw meat, prey animals, and labeled flasks. Clara is able to detect that at least some of the food and drink is laced with LSD or cocaine and avoid those while sampling something to appease him.
    • Upon him prompting her for her answer to his proposal regarding her working for him as his personal bodyguard, Clara accepts, which seems to pleasantly surprise him.
    • Clara inquires about how his power to subdue Death Rage works, and asks if he knows of any human possesses the power to incite Death Rage, which he denies. She also asks him if he knows Sister Cathwulf, which prompts him to request a "phone book" and information on contacts from one of his lackeys.
    • Clara admits that the situation with Sister Cathwulf is what prompted her to seek him out. He says he'll look into it, then takes $500 out of a nearby drawer and hands it to her -- she notices his hands are very dry as he does so -- telling her to consider it a bonus.
  • Dade pays a visit to Gregory Whistler. After explaining the events of the previous night, Whistler tells Dade that he reminds him of his late son, Alek. He takes a bottle of stiff whiskey from a drawer and pours a pair of shots for the two of them. After he promises to come up with something to help broker peace and Dade shuffles out of his office.
  • Paca heads home. On the way, she hears sirens and sees police cars. She senses Miguel and ducks into nearby bushes, shifting into Urhan form and attempting to eavesdrop as he speaks with officers.
    • The officer tells him that Paca is a wanted person and recounts her quitting her job, purchasing a firearm, and becoming involved in gang violence.
    • Upon hearing this, Paca slinks back through the bushes and makes a beeline for Pack HQ. She leaves a note there for the Pack asking them to take care of Miguel as she deals with some personal business, then disappears into the night.

Beats

  • Everyone gets 1 Beat for attendance.
  • Dade gains 1 Beat for completing the long-term aspiration of helping Little Shadow to break his bane and 1 Beat for investigating LeShaun White's murder, putting him at 64 Beats.
