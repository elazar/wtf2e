Summary

Wren receives a mission from LeShaun White and has a date with Miguel. Little Shadow shows his parents' home to Dade. Clara travels to the Shadow and visits with the White Agora, then meets Wren and Miguel for pizza. Paca meets with the leader of a local Hunter cell. The Pack discusses conducting a Sacred Hunt for Hanzer-gah Ninna.

Notes

  • Background music is Kai Engel - Snowfall (Intro) - Official Music.
  • It's Monday and currently a crescent moon.
  • Wren receives a text from LeShaun White: "Meet me in 15 minutes, or less if possible, at the Detroit Institute of Arts. Come alone."
    • The DIA is in known Cadillac Company territory.
    • LeShaun asks Wren, "Why are we lacking our Cahalith?" Wren explains that they're being "pulled along with another pack's shennanigans" and reports to LeShaun that the pack has "had a spirit declare war on them."
    • LeShaun warns them not to become too involved, and reminds them of their loyalty to the Cadillac Company. He asks that they tell him next time they feel the urge to "wander off."
    • Wren explains the "slight complication" of them dating Miguel and his relation to Paca. Looking rather conflicted at whether to laugh or be concerned, LeShaun merely says that Wren is "full of surprises," then instructs them to continue associating with the Pack and feeding intelligence to LeShaun.
    • LeShaun is aware of the incidents involving Lupus et Fidelis at the Smokey Bottle Bar and the disappearance of the original Crescent Shadows.
  • Little Shadow shows Dade the home of his parents.
    • He provides their names, Hellen and Jesse, and the street address of the house.
    • It takes a lot of effort from Little Shadow to avoid shifting out of Homid form.
      • See Breaking Points that count toward Flesh for Harmony 3 or lower (p. 104) on how to help him retain Homid form so he can meet his parents safely.
      • Some particular examples: refusing to participate in the Siskur-Dah for Hanzer-gah Ninna, staying out of the Hisil for one week to one month, using a silvered weapon against another werewolf (e.g. Julian Graves).
    • In case Little Shadow's parents want to keep him, Dade should figure out how that can be done safely. John Selbie or Gregory Whistler may be able to provide advice on this.
  • Clara travels to an isolated island glade in the Shadow known to the Forsaken in order to seek out the White Agora.
    • She shifts into Hishu form and enters the Spirit, where the glade appears as a void.
    • She meditates on her brother Alex. It's implied that her First Change may have been responsible for him being in the hospital.
    • The White Agora doesn't reveal itself to Clara, but speaks to her in mystical written form. Clara communicates that she wants to find Death Wolf in order to reach Mother Luna. When asked why, she ultimately confesses that she wishes to know why she is or must be Uratha and if or how she can stop being so.
    • The White Agora appears briefly as a figure in white robes. It tells Clara that, to get what she wants, she must sever ties with her pack, all Uratha, and their entire world. Clara would have to remain with the White Agora for training, then be passed her off to another teacher.
    • Clara says she needs time to think. The White Agora bids her farewell, and Clara reappears in the Flesh.
  • Paca reviews the contacts in the phone she stole from her would-be mugger, trying to sort out which of them might serve her purpose.
    • She finds that the woman has or had a connection to the Rock City Compact and a man's name: Rafeeq, the likely leader of the group.
    • There's a conversation about buying a truck with silvered headlights, flashlights, and stakes and making sure "the crackheads don't find their way into the basement like last time."
    • Paca sends a message to Rafeeq: "I heard you have some problems I may be able to help you solve. Meet me tonight. ~P" She specifies the location of a warehouse. Rafeeq receives the message and doesn't reply.
  • Dade texts Paca, who agrees to meet him at HQ later that night around 11 to join him on the Siskur-Dah for Hanzer-gah Ninna.
  • Wren and Miguel meet for a date.
    • Wren expresses concern about Paca and her feelings about their relationship. Miguel reassures them, saying he'll vouch for Wren with Paca if need be. Wren hopes it doesn't come to that. The two profess their love for each other.
    • Miguel also mentions his worries about Paca seemingly emotionally distant, the recent odd timings of her presence and absence, and the car going missing. Wren points out that Paca may not be forthcoming because she fears for Miguel's safety, which doesn't seem to convince Miguel.
  • Paca texts Clara to invite her on the hunt, with the implication that it entails joining the Pack. Clara responds, saying she'll think about it. Paca responds with a thumbs up.
  • Clara texts Miguel asking if they should meet for a tutoring session. In the end, Miguel invites Clara to join he and Miguel for pizza.
  • Paca meets with Rafeeq (which is apparently not his real name).
    • She returns the phone of his associate Cheryll to him; Paca apparently scared her pretty well, which Rafeeq says is unusual.
    • Paca explains that she needs extra money and offers to work for him. Rafeeq explains that he doesn't lead a gang and that his colleague Cheryll "doesn't get the memo sometimes," apologizing for her attempt to mug Paca.
    • Rafeeq claims that he does lead a local neighborhood watch that deals with both unseemly mortals as well as supernatural beings (e.g. vampires, werewolves, mages, ghosts).
    • Paca texts Dade and Clara to take a rain check on the hunt.
  • Dade explains the situation with Hanzer-gah Ninna to Little Shadow. The two agree to spend that evening patrolling the Shadow.
    • Dade spends 1 Essence (putting him at 9) to cross into the Shadow. Little Shadow is at Harmony 3 and can cross effortlessly.
    • As they cross, they meet Unseen Fire, who has seen what Hanzer-gah Ninna's presence have left behind in the Pack's territory in the Shadow. Unseen Fire leads them to the manhole covering the sewers where the Pack previously fought the Beshilu. In the Shadow, the manhole is huge and covered in First Tongue writings about the Beshilu.
    • Dade lifts the manhole cover, and the Pack detects the scent of smoke, fire, and burned rat. Unseen Fire believes he has grown more powerful to be able to do this on his own, more powerful even than she.
    • Unseen Fire leads Dade and Little Shadow to other places bearing more marks of burning and huge wolf paw prints scorched into the ground with fire. Spirits hide in fear as they pass.
