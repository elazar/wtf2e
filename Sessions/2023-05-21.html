Summary

Clara discovers some benefits of her new employment. Dade confers with Wise Lost on several matters concerning the Pack and digs into Sister Cathwulf's past.

Notes

  • Horace Fenchurch endows Clara with immunity from rage, which will last for a month if she loses contact with him. She converses with her new colleagues and learns about secret passages that they use within the Amber Club (to which this adds +2 to dice pools for related navigation rolls).
  • Dade travels to the library in the Hisil to confer with Wise Lost.
    • When asked about the black wolf spirit, Wise Lost points out that all Uratha are spirit in part, and without more specifics, it could've been present for any reason, including some unrelated to the pack.
    • Wise Lost seems afraid at the mention of a wound in the Hisil, and offers information on how to fix it, with no boon necessary, in exchange for a promise to heal the wound; Dade agrees. Wise Lost advises him to perform the Hunting Grounds ritual to claim the territory for the pack in both spirit and flesh, as a prerequisite for an ancient ritual to heal the wound.
    • Dade asks Wise Lost about Sister Cathwulf. The latter mentions Diana, a mortal of questionable sanity (and likely under the effects of lunacy) who visited the library some years ago to research Uratha.
    • When asked about sources of spontaneous rage in Uratha, Wise Lost replies that it will require research and the price of "finding knowledge freshest to arrive of yours, and destroy it utterly and completely."
  • Clara manages to unlock a door within the club and finds a room that contains a number of crafted items, some of them seemingly fetishes made by Uratha. Clara perceives echoes from these artifacts, seemingly tied to a single individual (presumably Horace Fenchurch), that cause her to lose her sense of how long she's been in the room.
  • Dade texts John Selbie and asks to meet; he agrees to do so that night.
  • Dade hacks into the library's system to access patron records. He finds a record of a Diana Cathwulf from several years ago and pulls up a list of the materials she checked out. He then expends 1 Willpower (putting him at 5) to take camera phone photos of about half of the list, but is caught in the act by a guard.
  • Dade uses the Strings of the Heart (p. 108) merit to discover that he wants to forget some awfulness that happened five years ago when he left a girlfriend. He offers him the means to forget, but shaken, the guard tells him to leave and threatens him if he loses his job because of this incident.

Beats

  • Everyone gets 1 Beat for attendance.
  • Dade gains 1 Beat for taking steps toward each of the aspirations of discovering the source of Sister Cathwulf's power and investigating the source of the wound in the Hisil and another Beat for stars and wishes, putting him at 73 Beats (14 XP 3 Beats).

Thoughts

  • For #stars-and-wishes :
    • Star: The fetish room visited by Clara was definitely interesting. Descriptions of it and its contents made me curious about what else might be in there.
    • Star: Wise Lost proved a very valuable informational resource for Dade. It was nice to see him be able to lean on someone than John Selbie or Gregory Whistler for help.
    • Wish: The relationship between Little Shadow and his parents has been developing very gradually up to this point. I'd like to see a more substantial progression in it.
    • Wish: I wouldn't mind seeing another scene with Miguel and Dade and/or Clara prior to Paca's return. Interactions between the former and other characters have been consistently interesting.
