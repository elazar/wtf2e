Summary

Clara arranges a play date between Socks and Germaine. Dade and Little Shadow gather Miguel from Paca's apartment. The pack reunites at Dade's apartment. Dade arranges for Little Shadow to visit his parents in a few days.

Notes

  • Clara awakens in HQ from her trip to Unseen Fire, then goes outside to find Germaine and offers to introduce him to her cat Socks. The two depart for Clara's place. When they arrive, Clara leaves Germaine outside and goes inside to find Socks. Germaine then enters and the two cautiously gauge each other. Socks then begins showing Germaine their favorite hiding places.
  • Dade and Little Shadow leave the graveyard. Dade texts Miguel to assess the situation with the police being at Paca's place, then he and Little Shadow go there. When they arrive, they see new spirits draped in black cloaks and fog that are difficult to identify and drawn to Miguel's emotions.
  • Dade expends 1 Essence (putting him at 6) to use Shadow Gaze (p. 115) on a grief spirit, which just finished devouring a night spirit, and learns that its bane is the song "Happy Birthday." He approaches the spirit and asks why it's there. It answers that someone grieves, a mortal touched by Luna grieving a lost love. Dade convinces the spirit to leave, then uses another 1 Essence (putting him at 5) to use Spirit Howl (p. 29) to scatter the remaining spirits.
  • Dade uses a key (we assume given to him by Paca) to let himself and Little Shadow in. Remaining spirits within the apartment have hidden themselves following the Spirit Howl. He finds Miguel upstairs, seemingly having started to pack things but given up halfway through to avoid a panic attack. He tries unsuccessfully to calm Miguel down, then ushers Little Shadow into the room to help, but he is also unsuccessful. Dade seems an owl spirit in the corner, who points out a song on a nearby CD. Dade pops the disc into a CD player and plays the track, which seems to help calm Miguel.
  • Miguel indicates that he would rather stay with Dade than at his house, and would like to see Clara. Dade texts her to that effect; she responds with a thumbs up. They depart from the house safely. Dade notices a silhouette of a black wolf sitting on the sidewalk that vanishes as quickly as it appeared. He texts John Selbie later, but he's unable to provide any further information on the spirit. During the car ride home, Miguel learns that Little Shadow now knows English, and the two get into an animated conversation.
  • Once Miguel and Little Shadow figure out sleeping arrangements, Dade speaks to Little Shadow and asks if he'd like to call his parents, then dials for him. It's apparently late enough that they were asleep, but his father comes to, shakes his mother awake, and they proceed to have a very tear-filled reunion over the phone. Little Shadow struggles with how to answer their questions about his life because of the Veil. Dade also brings up arranging a visit between them, and they confirm a date and time to meet two days later. Dade fulfills his short-term aspiration of arranging for Little Shadow to visit his parents.
  • Back at Clara's place, Socks and Germaine are getting on well. Clara gathers them into her car and drives them to Dade's apartment, where he lets them in. Clara and Miguel talk and she does her best to reassure him that everything will be okay.
  • Dade and Clara have a conversation in First Tongue. He explains the situation with the wound in the Hisii to her. She tells him about her new employment at the Amber Club.

Beats

  • Everyone takes 1 Beat for attendance.
  • Dade gets 1 Beat for fulfilling the short-term aspiration of arranging for Little Shadow to visit his parents and 1 Beat for stars and wishes, putting him at 69 Beats (13 XP + 4 Beats).
