Summary

Dade, Paca, and Miguel deal with the aftermath of Wren's visit. Clara visits the library and talks with Wise Lost. Clara and Dade learn more about Horace Fenchurch. Dade and Little Shadow visit Alex Cecil. Paca sneaks out for a date.

Notes

  • Dade and Paca discuss the situation with Wren briefly and ultimatley decide to let them go.
    • Dade tells Paca to let him know if there's anything he can do to help, then leaves to allow Paca and Miguel to speak amongst themselves.
    • Miguel still seems shaken by the odd encounter with Wren (see 2022-07-31); Paca explains that situations of this nature are part of why she hid her werewolf nature from him, and can see that Lunacy lingers in him.
  • Clara travels to the library, crosses over to the Shadow, and finds Wise Lost.
    • She asks for its help in discerning the nature of what Horace Fenchurch is. It agrees, for the price of another boon: find a stray animal and find it a home.
    • Clara agrees, and tells it what she's learned: Horace seems to have reptilian features, insisted that she eat his food, and has the ability to tranquilize Uratha.
    • Remaining in the Shadow, Clara interacts with one of the library computers, attempting to find more information on ways to mute her rage.
  • Dade returns to his apartment to make a steak breakfast for Little Shadow. They discuss the state of things with Miguel and Alex Cecil.
  • Dade does internet research on Horace Fenchurch.
    • That have been reports of some strange happenings at the Amber Club, such as people being persuaded to eat their own limbs.
    • Horace is the richest man in the city by far, has a reputation as a deal-maker, and is known for the quality and expense of his personal security force.
    • Horace is part of a family dynasty of sorts, a rather dysfunctional one in which its members compete to prove their worth for inheriting partriarchal family secrets.
    • Horace also has an interest in occult relics and tends to keep them fairly secure. He has contacts in the black market, financial institutions, government, high society, and police. He has no immediate family in the area.
  • Dade receives a call saying that Alex has woken up and is asking for him, that he's important somehow but he's unsure why, and that he's given descriptions of Paca and Elric. Dade visits him later that afternoon.
    • Alex Cecil has darker rings under his eyes and longer, messier hair than when Dade last saw him. He seems to have some vague residual memories of his sudden rise to and fall from fame (the latter of which he seems to believe was triggered by the Smokey Bottle Bar incident on 2021-04-25) and what happened in the Shadow on 2022-06-05.
    • After a prompt from Dade, Alex Cecil says that something offered him a pact, gave him exactly what he wanted, was with him all the time, was pleased with him, doesn't like Dade, and left now because of him. Dade gazes into the Shadow and sees an impression of a chain attached to his head, as if a spirit had been chained or fettered there, but the chain (of a violet red shade) was broken. A fettering typically happens to make a subject more susceptible to influence.
    • Dade searches for a spirit to question, but can't find a single one to target in the chaos of the hospital. He then questions Alex Cecil's nurse, who says he's been having nightmares, waking crying spells, and PTSD symptoms. Alex did recall hearing a baby cry on his doorstep and a bird (the Collector).
  • Clara spends a few hours communing with the data spirits in the library, trying to find more information about Horace Fenchurch.
    • She stumbles upon a journal written by a Uratha that she met at the Amber Club named Mark. It details his anguish stemming from his rage and people he's killed as a result, and points to involvement from Horace Fenchurch and him being a Host (p. 202-205), though it's not specific as to what kind.
  • Paca sneaks out (to avoid having to explain the situation to Miguel) for her date with Roger.
    • Paca shares that she's a guidance counselor; Roger shares that he's been a coach for about 10 years. Paca discovers that Roger knows a man by the name of Sergeant White, who's the local police force.
    • Paca took a rideshare to the date and asks Roger to take her home; he obliges. The two arrange for a second date the next week. She surprises him with a parting kiss.
