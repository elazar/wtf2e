Summary

Clara saves a kitten. Paca pays a visit to Horace Fenchurch at the Amber Club and receives a cryptic text from Rafeeq Jackson. Wren deals with their emotional aftermath from LeShaun White's death with Rebecca. Dade engages a raging Bridget Byreen in combat; Paca joins the fray.

Notes

  • It's been five days since the conversation between Clara and Horace Fenchurch.
  • Following her departure from the scene of LeShaun White's murder, Clara speaks with a black kitten with white paws, coaxes it to her home, makes formula, and feeds it. She returns to the place where she found the kitten to attempt to find its mother, but is unsuccessful.
  • Paca sends a group text to the Pack asking about their leads on Horace Fenchurch. Clara replies that he's a Host. Dade replies that he can keep Uratha from Death Rage, and that he has bodyguards of that nature in his employ.
  • Dade watches as the Cadillac Company calms and participates in a ritual to lift up the corpse of LeShaun White to move it for burial, and tails them at a respectable distance.
  • Paca approaches the Amber Club, but hears a voice saying in First Tongue, "I wouldn't go any further if I were you, sister." She shifts into Hishu form for the sake of having an open conversation, but is opposed by the hired guard at the entrance she happens to approach and saunters off. She withdraws to the front entrance and manages to be escorted inside.
  • Wren returns to Crescent Shadows HQ and is eventually joined by Rebecca. They remuninate on being cast out of the Cadillac Company, feeling some relief at being out from under the thumb of LeShaun White and Bridget Byreen, and having a chance to breathe and take refuge from thoughts of past and future in the present. Rebecca departs, leaving Wren to their thoughts.
  • Clara texts Dade asking for contact information for Gregory Whistler. Dade provides it, but cautions Clara to be careful in her dealings with him. She attempts to contact him, but is rebuffed and referred to his secretary.
  • Paca is invited to sample from the table of Horace Fenchurch's table, and pours a seemingly transparent wine of sorts in a fancy glass. Paca alludes to connections with the Night Watch and the recent murder of LeShaun White, and attempts to use them as a bargaining chip. Ultimately, Horace Fenchurch rebuffs her, but offers her free admission to the club as a consolation prize of sorts, and she is able to learn that he has contacts among the black market. Just before she leaves, she receives a text from from Rafeeq Jackson saying, "Stay away. They found us."
  • Dade tails the Cadillac Company as they approach a small building and bang on the door. Bridget Byreen shifts into Gauru and plunges through the wall. Yelling, screaming, and gunfire follow. Dade shifts to Urshul and pursues her, crashing through a window, shifting to Gauru, and swiping at her arm and shoulder with claws, dealing 8 Lethal damage. She voluntarily submits to Death Rage.
  • Paca shifts to Urhan and tries to find the Night Watch HQ, but has only ever been guided there blindfolded.
  • Dade uses claws and manages to blind the left eye of Bridget Byreen. He expends 1 Willpower (putting him at 4), but fails to deflect her attack and takes 3 Lethal damage. He manages to soak 1 Lethal damage from her subsequent attack.
  • Paca finds Dade, shifts into Gauru, and slashes at Bridget Byreen, but she soaks it.

Beats

  • Wren takes 1 Beat toward joining the Crescent Shadows for not joining in the Siskur-Dah of the Cadillac Company.
