Summary

The Pack seeks out more information about Lupus et Fidelis, and allies ahead of their meeting with the spirit court of Hope.

Notes

  • Dade and Elric consult with Unseen Fire. She suggests extreme caution if they choose to deal with the Wise Lost, a Magath who inhabits the library, used to be a spirit of a stray animal, absorbed the essence of all the knowledge there and it corrupted him. She suggests contacting a faith spirit for further knowledge of Lupus et Fidelis, or the community spirit for help due to its concern for the community's welfare. She also advises them to keep an eye on Muthgah Ninna, the guardian spirit of Little Shadow; he makes her very uneasy.
  • Dade takes 3 Essence from the locus, putting him at a full 10 for his current Primal Urge of 1. He and Elric use the locus to cross into the Shadow with Unseen Fire. The community spirit would be with the spirit court of Hope and is thus inaccessible until the Pack approaches them. Dade is unaware of any likely locations of a faith spirit outside of the church location that John Selbie gave them, which is dangerous.
  • Elric recalls a nearby Catholic church in the Pack's territory, where they may be able to find a faith spirit. Dade spends 1 Essence and uses Spirit Whispers (see Crescent Moon's Gift) to summon the spirit of a pipe organ within the church, asking it who if anyone has attachment to the Lupus et Fidelis cult, but it is a rank 1 spirit tied to its location and has no relevant knowledge.
  • They're then approached by a small spirit inhabiting a manila folder of records. Dade spends 1 Essence to use [Shadow Gaze] (see Crescent Moon's Gift) on the spirit, learning that its bane is fire, and uses Elric's lighter to convince it to give up any knowledge it has on congregation connections to Lupus et Fidelis for 1 Essence. It gives them two names: Gloria Stanze, which they recognize from the news report as Paca's victim from the Smokey Bottle Bar, and David Stanze, presumably a relative of hers, who left the church for the cult a few months ago. It also gives them an address for the Stanze household, which is in the downtown area and within the territory of the Pure.

  • She introduces herself as Hanzer-gah Ninna, Predator with the Flaming Jaws; her name and essence have changed from her devouring of a fire spirit. She watches over Little Shadow.
  • Future Elric also appears and greets Elric. The former reassures the latter that the future will hold good things from the pack visiting the spirit court of Hope, and says he has influence with them. Elric calls Dade and Paca to move to the next room for a private conversation, which offends Future Elric, who then fades into the Shadow.
  • The patron of Little Shadow's tribe visits him in a dream, expressing disappointment over Little Shadow's lack of prowess in the Hunt. He despairs over the Beshilu continuing to defile his old pack's territory. He says that if Elric spurs him into Garou form at the right time, when the pack confronts the Beshilu, he believes he can do so with them without fleeing.
  • Little Shadow has the ability to speak with animals. After the Pack tells him about the cult's local church and the former residence of a suspected cult member, he disappears to consult with an animal spirit group to see if he can convince them to perform some reconnaissance for the Pack.
  • The Pack has a brief experience of the Shadow's nature and proximity permeating themselves and their surroundings. Words pass on the wind from the tribe's patrons, the Firstborn.
  • While in the kitchen, Paca gets the sense that someone is watching her, and saw her kill Gloria Stanze. She has a vision reliving the murder, then approaches the mysterious robed woman, but begins howling with her, even in the presence of other bar patrons, as the woman prostrates herself before Paca before she runs into a nearby alley and claws into the stone wall until she collapses. Sagrim-Ur gives Paca the vision, and says, "I was not going to make you a mass murderer; not my child." They seem to know her intimately, calling her by name.
  • Dade has a vision and sees a storm outside and hears a message on the wind from Skolis-Ur: "Lead by example, if not by place. You are not weak; I've decreed it so. You will lead them well. Your prey is nearer than you think."
  • Elric has a vision and sees the Shadow and the Flesh in their state of constantly feeding each other in a loop, affecting each other endlessly, and can hear Kamaduis-Ur speaking to him: "The spirits are yours, the Shadow must be ruled. Keep to the balance and you will not go wrong. Beware an enemy who lies close."
  • Unseen Fire appears, saying she has spoken with the spirit court of Hope and they're willing to receive the Pack the following night. They plan on putting the Pack through a test of some kind. Little Shadow returns from the Shadow at about 2am and goes to sleep. Dade and Elric crash at Paca's.
  • The Pack experiences another vision saying "beware the invisible predator" and receives the Inspired condition. In the case of Dade, the message from Skolis-Ur is the source of the condition.
