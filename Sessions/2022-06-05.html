Summary

Wren goes on the hunt with the Cadillac Company for Gregory Whistler and Julian Graves. Dade, Paca, and Clara pursue Su Ubh into the Shadow to rescue Miguel and find Alex Cecil.

Notes

  • Wren travels to Cadillac Company HQ after having prepared for a hunt. LeShaun White has heard that a member of the Ivory Claws was having a "stag party" or something along those lines, and wants his Pack to crash it.
  • Wren has a Blood Talon ability to see the Renown of other Uratha during the Siskur Dah without them intentionally flashing them, in silver for Forsaken and flaming red for Pure.
  • The Cadillac Company has a lion spirit as their totem.
  • Wren rolls Perception (Composure + Wits) + Primal Urge (2 successes) and notices that spirits nearby are in too ordered a configuration and giving off a substantial amount of pain. LeShaun White dismisses it as normal given the involvement of the Ivory Claws.
  • Wren shifts instantly into Urhan form and project their senses (2 successes) across the Gauntlet.
    • They see a large golden retriever and hear an elderly-sounding man wishing congratulations to a man named Gregory (presumably Gregory Whistler). Gregory responds by thanking the other man, calling him Julian (presumably Julian Graves), and expresses that he will miss hunting, but now has a business to run.
    • Julian asks if Gregory will return, expressing concern that he will lose touch with his wolf side. Gregory flatly denies this and dismisses the concerns, saying that he takes a sabbatical from his work at least once a month.
    • Julian asks after Gregory doing business with the New Crescent Shadows pack, but Gregory refuses to indulge him, and reminds Julian that Gregory didn't sell him out to the pack even when they asked him to. The conversation shifts to more casual subjects, and Wren withdraws their senses from the Flesh. Wren has a grudge against them, the nature of which isn't entirely clear.
  • Wren relays what they've seen to LeShaun White. Samuel Hanselman seems to have the most Renown of the present pack, but is content to be second to LeShaun. After some discussion amongst the pack, Wren pops into the Flesh, taunts Gregory, and flees toward a known locus with the intent of leading the two of them into an ambush. While waiting for their pursuers, Wren pulls out their phone and makes a call, asking someone to wait for them a couple of blocks away; the person reluctantly agrees. Wren is in Urshul form at this point.
  • Shortly after, an older red wolf with lighter highlights and a black wolf with silver highlights appear and give chase. They quickly begin to catch up to Wren and, despite them shifting into Urhan form to widen the gap, they're overtaken, but manage to dodge and pull ahead again.
  • Having just seen Miguel be taken, Paca shifts, travels to a locus, and (despite some difficulty) travels through the Gauntlet. Just before, she texts Clara about what's happened. After crossing, she attempts to get a scent and senses that Dade and Little Shadow passed through here not long before. In Urhan form, she's able to catch up shortly after and quickly explains what happen. The three shift into Urshul form and speed back to Paca's house to try to find any spirits who may have seen Miguel's abduction.
  • Dade announces to the spirits near Paca's house that he'll award 2 Essence to a spirit who can give them information about Miguel's abduction. A lamp spirit saw that Su Ubh dragged Miguel away toward Center Pallace Shopping Center downtown, where Dade and Little Shadow were just heading before Paca found them. They thank the lamp spirit, return to HQ to meet up with Clara, and from there track Su Ubh.
  • The Pack finds multiple nests in the shopping center, the lower of which contain human remains. Paca manages to reach a nest and finds Alex Cecil looking incredibly malnourished. Dade uses a point of Willpower (putting him at 5), uses the Banish ritual, and prays to Luna to return Alex to the Umbra, causing his body to fade and disappear.
  • Clara discovers that the Collector takes humans touched by the supernatural (in the case of Miguel, Paca) with the intention of returning them to its nest to care for them, and does them no harm unless they attempt to escape, not unlike a magpie's affinity for shiny things. The Pack hears crying from another nest and Dade hears the flapping of wings moving faster toward them than a car. Su Ubh arrives and, upon seeing the Pack, flees.
  • Amongst human gore, Clara finds a locket containing photos of a father and son; a cracked mirror; the shirt, jeans, and skull of an adult human; moldly spirit remains and water; and a message written in blood onto the concrete support of the nest saying, "Sorry, Timmy. I couldn't get out. Miss you."
  • Dade expends 2 more Willpower (putting him at 3) to climb and manages to reach the second nest; Paca follows. They find Miguel in the second nest. Dade performs Banish on Miguel as well. The Pack returns to the Flesh. Dade calls 911 and requests an ambulance to their location to get Alex Cecil. Miguel finally awakens, realizes where he is and that his mother is there with him, and bursts into tears and falls into her arms. After a moment, she ushes him away, and Clara leaves as well.
