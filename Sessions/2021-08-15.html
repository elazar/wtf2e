Summary

Dade looks further into Julian Graves. Dade and Paca enter the sewers to pursue Mooth Hirabayu and the Beshilu.

Notes

  • Unsure of the nature of the spirit he encountered on 2021-08-01, Dade texts John Selbie and asks for a meeting. He replies to meet him Tuesday (in a couple of days) at the same location as last time (on 2021-05-16, at the old opera house) and to bring a pack of beer, a turkey sub, and some rose petals.
  • The Fire-Touched have taken the east side of Detroit as their territory, which contains a lot of churches. Dade goes near their territory to investigate Julian Graves and encounters a small group, who call him Reverend Graves and describe him as an occasionally intense street preacher, but otherwise sing his praises, and point him to a few locations where he gives sermons most inside their territory and one near it, but at random times.
  • Dade texts Gregory Whistler and requests that he call, intending to ask him about Graves, but only gets back that his text was read; Whistler doesn't reply.
  • Dade spends 1 Essence (putting him at 5) to activate the Shadow Hunter ability of his Crescent Moon's Gift for the duration of the current hunt for Mooth Hirabayu. Upon meeting at HQ, Dade and Paca take 5 and 4 Essence respectively (putting Dade at 10) from the locus there.
  • Tonight is a half moon. Next week is the Sunday of the full moon.
  • The pack enters the sewer where they last saw Mooth Hirabayu. There, they hear voices, speaking in First Tongue, one seemingly giving a sermon, another giving a sarcastic comment.
  • Paca and Dade throw molotov cocktails into the space where they encounter the Beshilu and Mooth Hirabayu.
    • Dade expends 1 Willpower (putting him at 5) to improve his effort, spends 2 Essence to reflexively shift into Urshul form, then bites the Beshilu and deals 3 Lethal damage.
    • A swarm of rats deal 1 Bashing damage to Dade which he heals next round.
    • Dade expends 1 Willpower (putting him at 4) and rakes the Beshilu with his claws, dealing 2 Lethal damage.
    • The Beshilu's form discorporates, dissolving into a hoard of rats, and it escapes.
    • Paca tears the body possessed by Mooth Hirabayu in half, slaying the Ridden.
  • Little Shadow is possessed by Hanzer-gah Ninna.
