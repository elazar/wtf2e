Summary

Wren does some housecleaning, has tea and visits with Clara, and naps at HQ. Clara acquires a useful new ability and has a disturbing vision. Dade and Clara investigate The Last Firstborn, unexpectedly encounter Sister Cathwulf, and leave a trail of gory destruction in their wake.

Notes

  • Wren is at HQ and spends an hour cleaning while waiting for the rest of the pack. Unseen Fire seems pleased with Wren for their contribution.
  • Dade ushers Clara and Paca away, and attempts to tend to Rafeeq Jackson's wounds.
  • As Clara gets outside of Night Watch HQ, she receives a phone call from Wren. Wren explains that they're hiding out at HQ because they were expelled from the Cadillac Company and their ambivalence at the deaths of LeShaun White and Bridget Byreen.
  • Once Dade has called 911 and seen to each of the members of the Night Watch, he leaves the scene and follows Clara and Paca to the Pack's HQ.
  • As Clara and Paca arrive at HQ, they find Wren laying face-down in the corner of the room. After a brief exchange, Clara goes to make some tea for Wren. As she does so, Wren uses the gift of Luna's Embrace (p. 136-137) to change their gender to male, then rises and follows after Clara.
  • Dade arrives at HQ to it looking cleaner and those present there having tea together. After the other Pack members attempt to reassure and comfort them, Wren goes to a nearby couch and falls asleep.
  • Dade and Clara discuss venturing to The Last Firstborn to investigate the leads provided by Gregory regarding Sister Cathwulf.
  • Clara has the ability to learn All Doors Locked (p. 135), but must learn it from a spirit. They cross into the Spirit, then travel to the office of Sweet Relief Security, whose slogan is, "It's all about trust, and we got that." The office exists as a heaily guarded fortress in the Hisil. Keys with arms and legs march around it, keeping watch. As Clara approaches, she is greeted by a spirit who calls itself the Keymaster. When she explains her request, and after an intense but short delay, the Keymaster obliges her for 2 Essence and putting a good word in for it with other security-focused spirits (as well as 3 XP).
  • Dade and Clara return to HQ, transition to the Flesh, and travel to The Last Firstborn by bus. When they arrive, Clara attempts to feign finding the restroom while looking for the basement entrance. She takes the Leveraged tilt (p. 308) and agrees to come back within three days and give the bouncer $5 for "directions to the bathroom." The bouncer opens the basement door for her, winks, and tells her to be quick. She moves out of Dade's line of sight down the stairs, and the door closest behind her. He uses Pack Awareness of keep tabs on her.
  • Clara descends into the basement and finds a bedroom, just as Gregory described. She is able to get into the adjacent room and finds it filled with wolf deity iconography. As she enters, she is greeted by the voice of Sister Cathwulf, who is kneeling in prayer, saying, "Welcome, my lady." In attempting to question her, Clara finds her to be genuinely unaware of her supernatural abilities, nor does she smell of being Uratha or Wolf-blooded.
  • Clara expends 1 Essence and uses Echo Dream (p. 125-126). She sees a vision of Sister Cathwulf, with spiderweb cracks running through her mind, walking confidently, amidst a small army being eviscerated by monsters. She kneels in supplication to a gigantic shadow of a wolf, but cast by a being not entirely like a wolf. Blood falls from this being, which Sister Cathwulf takes into herself, then releases back out Uratha, not under her command but perfected. The vision concluded, Clara falls into Death Rage.
  • After some cajoling, Dade convinces the bouncer to investigate the basement, then follows after him. After hearing the bouncer ask if everything is alright, and hearing Sister Cathwulf confirm it, Dade follows suit and falls into Death Rage himself.
  • After 10 minutes, Dade and Clara come to their senses. Dade finds he is covered in human blood; Clara has some as well, though less. They find a bullet lodged in the wall opposite them halfway up the staircase, and can hear police sirens around the building. The room behind them is wrecked. The bouncer is dead and Sister Cathwulf is gone.
  • Clara expends another Essence to use All Doors Locked again to find another exists in the vicinity and finds that the bedroom has a backdoor next to the closet. They take the door and leads into a back alleyway out of view of the police. Dade expends 1 Willpower (putting him at 0) to steathily move out of the alley with Clara.
  • Dade and Clara return to his apartment and clean themselves. Clara tells Dade about her vision. The two resolve to meet with the other Pack members before taking any further action against Sister Cathwulf or Horace Fenchurch.

Beats

  • Everyone gains 1 Beat for attendance.
  • Dade takes 1 Beat for taking notes, 1 Beat for completing the aspiration of investigating the Last Firstborn, and 1 Beat for #stars-and-wishes, putting him at 54 Beats.
